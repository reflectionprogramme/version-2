Memoria

Memoria tells of a flashback in the memory journey between two people who are unlikely to be reunited. The journey begins when the voice of a woman who identifies herself as 'I' appears and inquires about 'you', who she cannot see, over an old payphone, followed by a journey through the past disguised in a joint coin search mission. Something finally realized by the figure of 'I', that everything that has happened will not come back even if she waits for a long time and that 'I' and 'You' will not be destined to be 'Us'.
